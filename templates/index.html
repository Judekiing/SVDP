Welcome to St. Vincent de Paul

Welcome to the website for Saint Vincent De Paul, fondly known as the Old Cathedral. The Old Cathedral is one of the most historic buildings in Oyigbo with roots dating back to 1764 when St. Vincent founders Pierre Laclede and Auguste Chouteau dedicated a parcel of land to the people of St. Louis for religious purposes that was called the "church block". The present building is the fourth church to be erected on the site. Construction began in 1831 and the church was dedicated in 1834. Thanks for visiting our website. We hope you will come and pray with us soon!
